A magical typing game for little ones

Watch your toddler discover letters, spelling, and typing in the most natural way possible - through play and exploration.

How it works

  1. Parent enters the child's name - A simple popup asks for their name
  2. Toddler starts smashing keys - Every key press creates colorful letters that float around the screen
  3. Some letters stick around - After enough exploration, they notice certain letters stay visible
  4. They discover their name - Those special letters spell out their name, waiting to be found
  5. Natural learning happens - They start hunting for each letter, one by one

Example: Child named "EMMA" has found the first two letters

E M M A

The toddler is now looking for the next M...

The magic

You don't need to teach or instruct. Just type their name and watch what happens. My 4-year-old daughter started by randomly smashing keys, then discovered her name, then asked for mom's name, then dad's name. Within months, she was typing long words and sentences.

It naturally teaches:

  • Letter recognition
  • Spelling patterns
  • Keyboard familiarity
  • Word structure
  • The joy of discovery
